About PDX Rocks

Who

PDX Rocks is a Portland group interested in connecting climbers with climbers. We prefer a chill vibe and encourage all sorts of climbers but aren't really interested in hearing all about it brah, we just wanna get out and do it because we love it!

What

Connect climbers to facilitate all the climbing!!

When

As often as possible! Find folks with similar schedules.

Where

  • The PNW Great Outdoors!
  • Portland Rock Gym
  • The Circuit Boldering Gym
  • Planet Granite
  • The PSU Climbing Center

But not Multnomah Athletic Club, currently none of us are this fancy but would love to include MAC members in the group!

Why

Because it rocks!!

How

  • Indoor bouldering, top roping, lead climbing
  • Outdoor bouldering, top roping, lead climbing

We would love help developing a trad program!
